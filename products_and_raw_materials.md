# Classification of Final Products and Raw Materials - OilChem Application

This document clearly separates the **final products** (price forecasting targets in the application) and the **raw materials** (used as inputs/feedstocks).

---

## 1. Final Products (Forecasting Targets)
These products correspond to the selectable options in the application's user interface, for which price forecasts and margins are calculated:

1. **Butyl Acetate**
2. **Ethyl Acetate**
3. **Propyl Acetate**
4. **Isopropyl Acetate**
5. **Acrylic Acid**
6. **Phthalic Anhydride**
7. **Maleic Anhydride**
8. **Methyl Methacrylate (MMA)**
9. **Butyl Acrylate**
10. **Vinyl Acetate Monomer (VAM)**
11. **2-Ethylhexyl Acrylate (2-EHA)**
12. **Ethyl Acrylate**
13. **Acetone** (Route 1 & Route 2)
14. **Dibasic Ester (DBE)**
15. **Isopropyl Alcohol (IPA)**
16. **Methoxy Propyl Acetate (PMA / MPA)**
17. **Methoxy Propanol (PM)**
18. **Isophthalic Acid (PIA)**
19. **Purified Terephthalic Acid (PTA)**
20. **n-Butanol**
21. **Isobutanol**
22. **Methyl Ethyl Ketone (MEK)**
23. **Styrene**
24. **Toluene**
25. **Monoethylene Glycol (MEG)**
26. **Diethylene Glycol (DEG)**
27. **Propylene Glycol (PG)**
28. **Brent Crude** (Crude oil benchmark used as macro reference)

---

## 2. Raw Materials (Inputs / Feedstocks)
These raw materials and chemical intermediates are used upstream to manufacture the final products listed above:

* **Base Petrochemicals & Hydrocarbons:**
  * **Ethylene**
  * **Propylene**
  * **Benzene**
  * **Toluene** (also a final product)
  * **o-Xylene (Orthoxylene)**
  * **m-Xylene (Metaxylene)**
  * **Paraxylene (PX)**
  * **Naphtha / Reformed Naphtha**
  * **Cyclohexane**
  * **2-Butene & 1-Butene**

* **Base Alcohols & Acids:**
  * **Methanol**
  * **Ethanol**
  * **n-Propanol**
  * **Isopropanol / Isopropyl Alcohol** (also a final product)
  * **n-Butanol** (also a final product)
  * **Isobutanol** (also a final product)
  * **2-Butanol**
  * **Octanol (2-Ethylhexanol)**
  * **Acetic Acid**
  * **Dicarboxylic Acid**
  * **Acrylic Acid** (also a final product)

* **Other Intermediates & Energy:**
  * **Propylene Oxide**
  * **Methoxy Propanol (PM)** (also a final product)
  * **Acetone** (also a final product)
  * **Natural Gas / LNG** (energy & production cost indicator)
